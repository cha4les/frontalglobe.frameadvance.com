Frontal Globe

About

Geo-train your brain! The Frontal Globe app will help you learn the countries and capitals of the world.

Features:

  • Over 300 political regions (countries, dependencies, states, provinces, territories, etc.)
    • All 193 sovereign countries of the world and their capitals
    • All 50 US states and their capitals
    • All 13 Canadian provinces and territories, and their capitals
    • All 9 Australian states and territories, and their capitals
  • Incredibly detailed, 100% vector graphics (no blurry pixels)
  • Displayed as a globe, not flat projection, so features' shapes/sizes are not distorted
  • As you learn, your progress is highlighted on the globe
  • Customizable color schemes
  • No internet access required, so you can learn on the go

Coming soon to an iOS device near you!
